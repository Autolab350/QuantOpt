ENGINEERING BLUEPRINT FOR QUANTITATIVE STRATEGY ARCHITECT

PHASE ONE SYSTEM INTEGRATION BRIDGE
The first engineering step is to create a bidirectional data bridge between your Python data sources and the Rust performance engine. You use the GEX Computer FastAPI infrastructure to fetch real time ticker data option chains and GEX levels. This data is serialized into a standard JSON format that the OptionStratLib Rust engine can parse without manual configuration. This bridge ensures that the mathematical models are always working with fresh market reality rather than stale or mocked data.

PHASE TWO ANATOMIC RISK ENGINE INITIALIZATION 
You configure the Rust engine as a dedicated microservice that listens for strategy requests. When a ticker is identified by the Earnings Volatility Calculator the system triggers the Rust engine to calculate the full anatomy of the trade. This includes first order greeks for basic profit tracking and second order greeks like Vanna and Vomma to identify hidden volatility risks. The service maintains a strict threshold for your ten thousand dollar capital by instantly rejecting any strategy where the Monte Carlo simulated max loss exceeds two percent of your total account value.

PHASE THREE STRATEGY RECOMMENDATION AND LLM AUDIT
The system uses the Rust StrategyProbabilityAnalysis module to calculate the probability of profit expected value and profit area for twenty five different strategy types. These hard numbers are then fed back to the language model as a structured report. The language model acts as the strategic architect by reviewing the technical data against broader market conditions. If the Rust engine proves that a specific Iron Condor has an eighty five percent probability of profit and a positive expected value the LLM generates the human readable recommendation to enter the trade.

PHASE FOUR PORTFOLIO GROWTH AND PREMIUM MANAGEMENT
To achieve the goal of growing ten thousand to fifteen thousand dollars the system implements an automated premium collection loop. It targets high Theta decay windows specifically identifying four week durations where time value erodes the fastest. The engineering focus is on consistency rather than high risk spikes. By prioritizing strategies with high probability of profit and using GEX walls as protective barriers the system automates the process of picking trades with the highest statistical reassurance and lowest structural fragility.

PHASE FIVE USER INTERFACE AND FEEDBACK LOOP  
The final component is a monitoring dashboard that visualizes the trade anatomy in real time. It displays the payoff curves generated by Rust and overlays them with live price action relative to your break even points. This creates a continuous feedback loop where you as the operator can see exactly why a strategy was chosen and how the high order greeks are currently protecting your lifeline capital. The system is engineered to be a defensive shield that only allows you to proceed when the math and the strategy architecture both indicate a superior win rate.
